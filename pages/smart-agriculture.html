From Traditional to Transformational

Moving farmers away from low-profit traditional crops toward high-value agriculture with digital education and market access

This program revolutionizes farming in Western Kenya by shifting from low-profit crops like maize, sugarcane, and beans to high-value alternatives like soybeans and African Bird's Eye Chili.

Through digital mentorship, market linkages, and value addition training, we're creating sustainable pathways to higher incomes and food security for thousands of farming families.
