Modern Skills for Modern Jobs

Container-based digital learning centers placed strategically across Bungoma County for maximum accessibility

Our Digital Skills Hubs Network transforms shipping containers into state-of-the-art learning centers, making modern tech education accessible, fast, and job-oriented for youth across Western Kenya.

Located in schools, churches, trading centers, and public spaces, these hubs provide practical training that leads directly to employment opportunities in the digital economy.
