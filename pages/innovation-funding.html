How the Platform Works

Six-step process from idea submission to successful scaling

1

Application or Nomination

Local youth or community groups submit ideas online or via physical forms at Digital Hubs

Online Form Hub Submission Community Nomination
2

Shortlisting & Mentorship

Promising ideas receive mentorship on refining pitch, business model, and execution strategy

Business Mentors Pitch Training Model Refinement
3

Match with Funders

Platform algorithm matches ideas to investor interests in agriculture, tech, climate, and other sectors

AI Matching Sector Alignment Investor Profiles
4

Pitch Events

Virtual Pitches Physical Events Investor Meetings
5
